Taiwan's 百岳 Baiyue

In 1971, a group of Taiwanese hikers compiled a list that's since become known as the 百岳 Baiyue. It's a collection of 100 peaks above 3000 m. As such it has become a bucket list for many Taiwanese hikers.

In 1992, in an effort to promote national mountaineering, the Sports Committee of Taiwan identified 100 entry-level hikes. These peaks are known as the 小百岳 Xiaobaiyue, Taiwan's 100 "little" peaks.
